*Power Storage

Storing power for use at a later time is a great way at preventing circuits from going offline should the main power source reduce or stop producing power. For a more detailed explanation, check out Power Storage in the Concepts section.

Notes:

  • Small Batteries are very compact and can be placed on some deployables like work/repair benches and boxes.
  • Maximum power input is 40rW.
  • Comes with a default charge of 37rWm.
